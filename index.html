Frequently asked questions

What certificates can I track?

Gas safety (CP12), EICR, EPC, fire risk assessment, insurance, legionella checks, platform fire-safety uploads, and other dates you add manually.

Is this for long-term landlords?

HostCert is designed for short-term and holiday let hosts. Long-term BTL landlords may prefer broader landlord suites — we're focused on the self-managing STL host.

Do you integrate with Airbnb or Booking.com?

Not in v1. You enter dates once; we remind you before renewals and help you keep an inspection-ready record.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe customer portal or by emailing support@hostcert.co.uk.

What happens after I pay?

You'll complete a short onboarding form with property and certificate details. We set up your dashboard within 24 hours and send your welcome email.
